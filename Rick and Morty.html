Rick and Morty

Rick and Morty is a sci-fi cartoon created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon and produced by Atomic Cartoons. It is about Morty Smith, his family, and his grandpa Rick- a mad scientist who created a portal allowing him to travel anywhere and everywhere. The series follows them through their crazy adventures to different dimensions, planets and fights with Rick's numerous enemies.

I like this show because:

  • I find it funny that Justin Roiland voices so many of the characters and loads of the dialogue is so chaotic and sounds so unscripted but it works so well.
  • It's quite random and different from lots of other things.
  • It's funny and is easy to watch and re-watch.
