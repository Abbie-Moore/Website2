Hannibal

Hannibal is a TV series with characters and elements based on the books "Red Dragon" "Hannibal" and "Hannibal Rising" by Thomas Harris.

The series follows FBI profiler Will Graham who is used by the FBI for extreme serial killer cases because of his ability to empathise with the killer by analysing the gruesome crime scenes they leave behind. Dr Hannibal Lecter is a psychatrist, ex-surgeon, and (unbeknownst to the FBI) the serial killer known as the "Chesapeake Ripper" who the FBI are desperately trying to catch.

Jack Crawford, head of the FBI's BAU, hires Dr Lecter to supervise Will Graham as analysing horrific crime scenes starts to mess with his mind. This role lets Dr Lecter maniuplate the FBI from the inside. He is intrigued by Will's ability to think like the killers he catches and takes advantage of his role in Will's life to slowly break Will's sanity and try to turn him into a killer.
