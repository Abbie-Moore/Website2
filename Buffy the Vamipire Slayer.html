Buffy the Vampire Slayer

Buffy the Vampire Slayer is a series about Buffy Summers, a teenage girl who is destined to defend the earth against vampires, werewolves, and the forces of darkness- she is the Slayer.

The series begins with Buffy moving to a new town and hoping that her destiny would somehow not follow her. She quickly finds out that the town is on a "Hellmouth" and is the center of lots of supernatural evil. The librarian at her new school- Rupert Giles- informs her that he is her Watcher- the one who takes care of the Slayer and trains her so she can defend herself and the earth against evil. Buffy's identity as the Slayer is supposed to be a secret but that doesn't last for long and so BUffy and her friends who know her secret form a group called the "Scooby Gang" and they work together to fight off evil.
